## 🧠 Soft Skills & Career Strategies

I have spent time developing the "human" side of engineering. I now feel ready to enter the job market with these skills:

### 1. Career Preparation

- **CV & Portfolio:** I know how to present my skills and projects to attract recruiters.
- **Interview Mastery:** I can handle technical and behavioral interviews with a calm and professional attitude.

### 2. Teamwork & Communication

- **Active Listening:** Ensuring I understand my team’s needs before suggesting solutions.
- **The "I'm OK, You're OK" Stance:** Maintaining a positive, assertive relationship with colleagues and managers.
- **DIVA Method:** Controlling my voice (volume and rhythm) to be a clear and effective communicator.
